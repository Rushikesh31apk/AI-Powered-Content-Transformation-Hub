Tips & Tricks

Make the most out of your AI Hub experience

Better Summaries

For best results, paste well-structured text with clear paragraphs. The AI works best with content over 100 words.

Audio Quality Matters

Upload clean audio without background noise for accurate speech-to-text transcription. WAV and MP3 work best.

Clear Images for OCR

Use high-resolution images with good lighting. Avoid blurry or skewed photos for the best text extraction results.

Upgrade for More

Paid users get access to multiple premium voices in TTS, higher usage limits, and priority processing speed.

TTS Best Practices

Use proper punctuation and shorter sentences for the most natural-sounding audio output from text-to-speech.

Stay Secure

Your data is processed securely. Always log out after use on shared devices and keep your password strong.
